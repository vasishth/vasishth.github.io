About me

I'm professor of linguistics at the University of Potsdam, Germany. See my official home page. I have a BA (Honours) in Japanese (JNU, New Delhi), master's degrees in Linguistics (JNU), Computer Science (Ohio State, USA) and Statistics (Sheffield, UK), and a PhD in Linguistics (Ohio State).
